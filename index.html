About Me

Nick Mallory - Toronto, ON

I have been playing games for as long as I can remember. As I grew older my obsession moved from not just playing games, but to programming them too. Ever since then I just want to make people feel the same joy I get from playing games through developing them.

My other obsession would definitely be web development. This very website is an example of something I have so much fun creating. I find it incredibly rewarding to design a website and see it come to life with content. In college I learned about it, but it wasn't until afterwards that I came to truly appreciate it.
